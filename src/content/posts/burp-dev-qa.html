Burp for Dev and QA Teams

The highest-leverage AppSec move isn't a better scanner. It's putting the scanner where the people who can actually fix things already are.

What changed

QA already runs regression passes before every release. Wiring Burp into that existing workflow — instead of asking for a new one — meant findings showed up as failed test cases, not security tickets nobody owns.
